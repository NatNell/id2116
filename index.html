A beaver is a kind of large rodent. It is semi-aquatic, meaning some of the time it lives in water, some of the time it lives on land. Beavers can only be found in North America and Europe. Beavers are known for building and keeping dams in rivers.

Update 05

Trying out our revamped DJ Ham Soundstage.

We redesigned and printed out the back panel as the original plastic part was causing the wheel to tilt down.

The middle plastic part holding the main screw is thicken to reduce flexing.

Before printing out our CAD mockup, we decided a simple model of our envisioned Soundstage with some legos. Through this simple mockup, we can plan the positions of our switches and housing space for the microbit and wires. Likewise, the LED strips will be housed within the revised back housing design. .
